About Me
Computers are capable of amazing power that can be leveraged to move society forward. I'm a passionate engineer with a vision of harnessing such power to not only improve productivity, but also create new excitement for the world!

Since I began tinkering with computers in middle school, I have gained an entrepreneurial, detail-oriented energy, a tendency towards exploration and experimentation, and a quickness of adaptation to the ever-evolving technological landscape. Although Software Development soon emerged as my favorite area of IT, my interest in computing continues to drive me to learn, experiment, and practice in many areas of the field rather than just this one.

Because my experience spans several different niches in IT, I can help your team craft the best-fitting solution by examining the problem through multiple aspects of computing. Diversification of experience on a team is essential because it ensures the product it is seen by a variety of technical perspectives. This results in a product that is most accurate, secure, maintainable, scalable, and easy to use and importantly, one that does not fall short in any of these areas.

My work style involves mixing new research and my prior technical knowledge with a novelty-centric sense of creativity. I regard usability as the guiding principle of my work, with necessity being the catalyst.
